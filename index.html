Beer Tab App

Bar Menu and Tab App

The fast and easy way to order beer and drinks at the bar throughout the night. You can keep track of everything you have orderd throughout the night and also easily order another drink from your current tab. It makes paying at the end of the night hassel free, as you can just pay when your ready through your phone at any time.
